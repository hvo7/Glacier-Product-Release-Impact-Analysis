# Glacier-Product-Release-Impact-Analysis

Glacier is a mid-sized B2B SaaS company that provides a subscription-based workflow and collaboration platform to help small and medium business. 

Glacier recently released a new product update that introduced several new features to enhance user experience and engagement while reducing customer churn. 
However, after the release, executive opinions vary on whether the new release resulting in positive customer sentiment and improved business outcomes. 

This in-depth analysis was performed to compare business compare product utilization and customer outcome before and after the product release to:
* how customers reacted to the new feature
* how customer churn was affected by the release
* whether revenue increased were affected
